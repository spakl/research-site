Reaction Mechanism Searches

Experimental chemists can see what goes in and out of a reaction and propose a plausible mechanism. The individual steps and intermediates can be difficult or impossible to observe directly. Computational chemists can simulate these reactions to identify intermediates, transition states, and the energetics which are used to confirm these proposed mechanisms or propose the actual mechanism when it's an open question.
